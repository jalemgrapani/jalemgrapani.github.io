About Filipino Cuisine

Filipino cuisine is a vibrant blend of flavors and traditions that showcases the Philippines' rich cultural heritage. Influenced by various cultures, including Spanish, Chinese, and American, it features a fusion of indigenous ingredients and cooking techniques. At its core, Filipino cuisine relies on fresh, local ingredients such as rice, seafood, meats, and vegetables, enhanced by sauces like soy sauce, vinegar, and fish sauce. Signature cooking methods include marinating, grilling, stewing, and frying, resulting in distinctive savory and aromatic flavors.
